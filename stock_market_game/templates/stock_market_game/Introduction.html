Game Instructions

Welcome to the Stock Market Investment Game. Here's how it works:

Game Rules:

  • You will play 4 rounds
  • In each round, you start with ${{ Constants.initial_balance }} cash balance
  • Each round has 20 ticks, representing time periods
  • At each tick, you can invest money in the stock market or withdraw from your investment
  • The money you invest will fluctuate with the stock price
  • Your money not invested will remain stable in your cash balance

How to Play:

  • Enter a positive number to move money from your balance to investment
  • Enter a negative number to withdraw money from your investment
  • You have 60 seconds to make each decision
  • If you don't make a decision in time, no change will be made
  • Your final payoff for each round is the sum of your cash balance and investment value

Are you ready to begin? Click "Next" to start the first round.

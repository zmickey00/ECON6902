Experiment Instructions
Welcome to the Investment Decision Experiment

In this experiment, you will make investment decisions over 20 rounds.

Basic Rules:

  • You will receive $10 of initial capital in each round.
  • You will decide how much to invest in a stock based on price information.
  • The money you invest cannot be withdrawn until the end of the experiment.
  • Remaining balance from the previous round cannot be carried over.
  • At the end, your total earnings will be the sum of all uninvested money plus the value of your stock holdings at the final price.
